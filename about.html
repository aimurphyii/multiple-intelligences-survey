Who are we?

Adrienne Easton

Adrienne Easton is a JavaScript Software Developer who has also worked as school psychologist, psychometrist, and educational specialist. She came up with the idea for this project having given this informal survey to many students over the years to help empower them, their parents, and their teachers to understand and embrace their strengths, beyond just formal IQ and academic testing. Besides writing code and administering psychoeducational and neuropsychological tests, Adrienne also enjoys acting, singing, swimming, and making balloon animals.

Adrienne's strongest intelligence is: Intrapersonal.

Aileen Murphy

Aileen, like her colleagues, is a software developer based out of Seattle. This girl has lived around the globe and done everything from selling scuba gear, to teaching 4th grade science, to briefing generals--yes, like, military decision makers. These ragtag experiences have shaped beliefs in her heart but two of the strongest are: there's no place like home and leave it better than you found it. She has deep convicitons about education and our natural environment, since she plans to leave this place to her kids. The influence of these convictions and her belief in the vision of her friend, Adrienne, drew her to join this team.

Aileen's strongest intelligence is: Musical.

Haley Lewis

Haley Lewis is a front end web developer. She previously worked in corporate media production, reality television and independent film. She was inspired by this project because, as an empath, she has a passion for helping people discover more about who they are as people and play to their strengths. In addition to coding, Haley enjoys most TV shows, crafting, cooking, playing with her adorable fur babies, and planning her wedding.

Haley's strongest intelligence is: Interpersonal.

He wanted to be apart of this project becasue when he heard that it would be used for schools and a real life aplication, he loves to be a part of projects that affect peoples lives for the better. Along with writing code for the project by touching each type of langauge he also enjoys snowboarding, F1 racing, drawing, and ultimate frisbee.
